How to Use the Academic Research Assistant

Getting Started

Enter an academic research topic in the input field and click "Begin Research" to start.

Research Plan

Review the automatically generated research plan. You can modify it if needed before starting the research process.

Research Results

View your research results in three formats:

  • Content: The full research text with proper academic formatting.
  • Visualizations: Charts and graphs generated from the research data.
  • Citations: All sources cited in the research with proper academic formatting.

Downloading Research

Click the "Download as Markdown" button to save your research as a formatted markdown file.

Comparing Research

Use the comparison feature to compare multiple research topics side by side.
